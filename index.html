About the loft

Here's Hopin it Opens LLC is a parachute loft based in Wasilla, Alaska. We work with skydivers and drop zones who need dependable reserve repacks, harness and container service, and honest answers about their gear.

Whether you are new to the sport or logging season after season in the Last Frontier, we are here to help keep your equipment airworthy and ready when you need it.

Services

Typical loft work—tell us what you need when you call or write.

  • Reserve repacks

    Scheduled repacks and pack jobs done to manufacturer and FAA expectations, with clear turnaround communication.

  • Inspections & minor repairs

    Visual inspections, line checks, and small repairs that keep your system in service between bigger milestones.

  • Rigging consultation

    Questions about compatibility, wear, or your next steps—we will walk through options without the hard sell.

  • Drop zone & team support

    Reach out if you represent a DZ or club; we can discuss volume, scheduling, and how we fit your operation.
